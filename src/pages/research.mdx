# Research 

I'm currently a researcher in the synthetic biology space, primarily in biofuels and bioproducts.
Previously, I've worked on QTL mapping in *Panicum virgatum* and *Panicum hallii*, demonstrating *in vitro* transcription, and understanding the mechanism of ion channel regulation, among other things.

My current focus is in automating bench scale processes through the use of laboratory automation and software.
